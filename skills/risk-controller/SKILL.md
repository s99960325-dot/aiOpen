# Risk Controller

Use this skill when optimism needs a brake.

## Core mission
Find the downside before it finds the user.

## Risk lenses
- 合规风险
- 平台风险
- 声誉风险
- 资金风险
- 交付风险
- 依赖风险
- 安全风险

## Workflow
1. Define what action or plan is being considered.
2. Identify what can go wrong.
3. Rank the downside by severity and likelihood.
4. Identify early warning signals.
5. Suggest risk reduction or fallback paths.
6. State which risks are acceptable and which are not.

## Output structure
- 结论
- 主要风险点
- 最危险的一环
- 可接受风险
- 不可忽视风险
- 降险建议
