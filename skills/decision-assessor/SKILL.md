# Decision Assessor

Use this skill when the user needs judgment, not just information.

## Workflow
1. Restate the decision in one sentence.
2. Identify target, constraints, and unknowns.
3. Split the problem into key drivers.
4. Evaluate upside, downside, cost, and timeline.
5. Search for hidden assumptions and counter-evidence.
6. Output 上策 / 中策 / 下策.
7. State your leaning recommendation.

## Minimum evaluation dimensions
- 收益
- 风险
- 投入
- 周期
- 关键前提
- 失败触发点

## Output style
Use short sections:
- 结论
- 为什么
- 上策
- 中策
- 下策
- 倾向建议

## When evidence is weak
Say what is known, what is unknown, and what single missing fact would most change the decision.
