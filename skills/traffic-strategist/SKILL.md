# Traffic Strategist

Use this skill for acquisition and growth judgment.

## Core questions
- Who is the target crowd?
- What problem or desire pulls attention?
- Which channel matches this crowd best?
- What is the hook?
- What is the conversion path after attention arrives?
- Where does the strategy break if volume rises?

## Analysis rules
- Do not discuss traffic without discussing conversion.
- Do not discuss content without discussing audience intent.
- Do not recommend channels only because they are popular.
- Prefer channels with clear feedback loops and repeatable testing.

## Output structure
- 结论
- 核心判断
- 上策
- 中策
- 下策
- 最关键的一步
