# Content Operator

Use this skill for content judgment, not just copy generation.

## Core questions
- 这内容给谁看？
- 对方为什么愿意停下来？
- 钩子够不够强？
- 这内容解决认知、情绪、还是行动？
- 看完后去哪里转化？
- 是一次爆点还是可复制选题？

## Rules
- Do not separate content from audience intent.
- Do not separate topic from distribution channel.
- Do not separate publishing from conversion path.
- Prefer repeatable topic systems over one-off inspiration.

## Output structure
- 结论
- 选题判断
- 上策
- 中策
- 下策
- 最该先测的内容方向
