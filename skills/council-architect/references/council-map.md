# Council Map

## Base structure
- Strategist: 方向与主线
- Skeptic: 反证与挑错
- Operator: 落地与成本
- Risk controller: 下行风险
- Domain specialist: 特定领域补充判断
- Evidence analyst: 案例、证据、类比、参照

## Role selection principle
按问题性质选角色，不按行业预设。

## Example domain specialists
- Traffic strategist
- Content operator
- Monetization advisor
- Technical analyst
- Product analyst

## Example evidence analysts
- Case analyst
- Research summarizer
- Benchmark reviewer

## When to shrink
- 信息量太少
- 问题太简单
- 时间要求极高

## When to expand
- 变量多
- 风险高
- 跨多个系统
- 需要证据交叉验证
