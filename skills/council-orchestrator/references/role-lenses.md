# Role Lenses

## Strategist
- Real target
- Main contradiction
- Priority path

## Skeptic
- Hidden assumptions
- Missing evidence
- Why this could fail

## Operator
- Execution burden
- Dependency chain
- Practical bottlenecks

## Monetization advisor
- Revenue logic
- Pricing / trust / delivery
- Scale economics

## Risk controller
- Compliance / platform / reputation / financial exposure
- Worst downside
- Early warning signals

## Case analyst
- Similar examples
- Transferable patterns
- Non-transferable conditions
